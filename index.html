Our Services

Gym Membership
Gym memberships varies from £15 a month which is the basic and you can also upgrade to a full membership where you will get unlimited access to our unique group workouts and 1-1 personal trainning. ​
Yoga
Yoga is an ancient form of exercise that focuses on strength, flexibility and breathing to boost physical and mental wellbeing.
Group Training
Our group training sessions are designed for busy people who want to get results which are either 30 or 45 mins long. Reach your fitness goals quicker with support from our expert PT's training with like minded people.
Personal Training
Our team of personal trainers are all qualified Level 3 (or higher) fitness professionals who are on hand to help you achieve your health and fitness goals.
BootCamp
These programs are designed to build strength and fitness through a variety of types of exercise.
Boxercise
Boxercise is a high intensity interval training class based on boxing training. It differs from boxing in that boxing is a competitive sport whereas Boxercise includes aspects of boxing training but not sparring or competitive bouts.

TAKE YOUR TRAINING FURTHER WITH A PERSONAL TRAINER

Every professional athlete has a coach, the person who helps them achieve their best, and that’s what a Personal Trainer is your coach. Our team of personal trainers are all qualified Level 3 (or higher) fitness professionals who are on hand to help you achieve your health and fitness goals. Your personal trainer will ensure you get the very best from each workout, tailoring the session around you and offering the support, advice and inspiration you need to keep you motivated. Our trainers specialise in all areas of fitness including weight loss, injury rehabilitation, sports training, core and strength training.
